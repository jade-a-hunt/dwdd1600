Our Story

Founded in 2017, Hearth & Hideaway began as a passion project between two lifelong travelers, Elena Carter and James Holloway. Inspired by their love for quaint villages, misty mountains, and secret getaways, they set out to create a travel agency that focused on slow, meaningful, and immersive travel experiences. What started as a small travel agency soon blossomed into a trusted brand for wanderers seeking charm, authenticity, and a touch of magic.

Popular Destinations

  • The Scottish Highlands & Isle of Skye: Where misty peaks meet historic castles and cozy inns.
  • The Cotswolds, England: A storybook countryside dotted with stone cottages and winding lanes.
  • Alsace, France: Half-timbered villages, cobblestone streets, and warm, welcoming wine taverns.
  • Hallstatt, Austria: A lakeside village with mountain charm and breathtaking winter scenery.
  • Dingle Peninsula, Ireland: Rugged cliffs, hidden beaches, and traditional Irish warmth.
  • The Black Forest, Germany: Enchanting woodlands, historic hamlets, and fairy-tale vibes.
  • Lapland, Finland: A magical winter wonderland with northern lights and cozy cabins.
  • Lofoten Islands, Norway: Red fishing huts, dramatic fjords, and the Arctic's serene beauty.
