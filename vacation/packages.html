Our Packages

Package One: Lakeside Serenity in Hallstatt, Austria

Destination: Hallstatt, Austria

Nestled between the majestic Dachstein Alps and the serene waters of Lake Hallstatt, this picturesque village offers a peaceful and romantic retreat. As a UNESCO World Heritage site, Hallstatt is known for its charming historic architecture, breathtaking lake views, and rich cultural heritage. Spend your days exploring the charming village streets, visiting the historic salt mines, and taking in the stunning alpine scenery with a boat ride across the tranquil lake.

Hotel: Heritage.Hotel Hallstatt

A beautifully restored lakeside hotel offering elegant rooms with panoramic views of Hallstatt's stunning landscapes. Guests can enjoy an authentic Austrian breakfast each morning while overlooking the picturesque lake.

Cost: $3,200+

Starting at $3,200 per person. This package includes five night accommodation, daily breakfast, a guided tourof Hallstatt, and a visit to the famous salt mines.

Rental Car: Rent a Car

Provided by Sixt Rent a Car for easy transportation from nearby cities like Salzburg or Vienna.

Package Two: Alpine Retreat in Gimmelwald, Switzerland

Destination: Gimmelwald, Switzerland

Escape to the breathtaking Swiss Alps in the idyllic, car-free village of Gimmelwald. This secluded haven is perfect for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful retreat surrounded by stunning mountain scenery. Enjoy hiking along scenic trails, taking in panoramic views of the Jungfrau region, and experiencing the charming, slow-paced lifestyle of this alpine gem.

Hotel: Hotel Pension Gimmelwald

A cozy, family-run inn with rustic charm, offering warm hospitality and spectacular views of the surrounding peaks. Guests can unwind in comfortable accommodations and enjoy traditional Swiss cuisine in the hotel's quaint restaurant.

Cost: $2,800+

Starting at $2,800 per person. This package includes five night accommodation, daily breakfast, and guided hikes through the stunning mountain trails.

Rental Car: Not Applicable

Not applicable, as Gimmelwald is a car-free village. Guests will arrive via scenic train and cable car transport, included in the package.

Package Three: Countryside Charm in the Cotswolds, England

Destination: The Cotswolds, England

Step into a storybook landscape of rolling hills, honey-colored stone cottages, and charming villagesv in the heart of England's countryside. The Cotswolds is the perfect retreat for travelers looking to experience traditional English charm, explore historic manor houses, visit local markets, and indulge in afternoon tea at cozy tearooms.

Hotel: Cotswold House Hotel & Spa

A luxurious boutique hotel offering elegant accommodations and a serene spa experience in the heart of the charming village of Chipping Campden.

Cost: $3,000+

Starting at $3,000 per person. This package includes five night accommodation, daily breakfast, a spa treatment, and guided tours of some of the most picturesque villages in the Cotswolds.

Rental Car: Hertz Car Rental

Provided by Hertz Car Rental for a scenic drive through the rolling hills and charming countryside lanes.
